In the event of cancellation of registration, adjustment of fees will be follows:

1. If the student has never attended classes, even for one (1) day, he/she will be charged only the registration fee in full.

2. If the student has attended classes, but withdraws in writing within the first (1st) week of classes, he/she will be charged ten percent (10%) of the total amount due for the term plus the registration fee in full.

3. If the student withdraws in writing within the second (2nd) week of classes, he/she will be charged twenty percent (20%) of the total amount due for the term plus the registration fee in full.

4. If the student withdraws in writing in the third (3rd) or fourth (4th) week of classes, he /she will be charged fifty percent (50%) of the total due for the term plus the registration fee in full.

5. If the student withdraws in writing after the fourth (4th) week of classes, he/she will be charged the total amount due for the term. The student will either be dropped, transferred or dismissed, and he/she will have to pay for the whole term.
